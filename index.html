About us

Tom Brughmans is a Leverhulme Research Fellow on the Oxford Roman Economy Project. Iza Romanowska is Senior Researcher at the Barcelona Supercomputing Center. Shawn Graham is Associate Professor of Digital Humanities at Carleton University. Together we have nearly forty years of experience with simulation and complex systems modeling of the Roman world. With this project, we wanted to try some analog simulation of Romans instead.

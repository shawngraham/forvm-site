About us

Tom Brughmans is a Leverhulme Research Fellow on the Oxford Roman Economy Project. Iza Romanowska is Senior Researcher at the Barcelona Supercomputing Center. Shawn Graham is Associate Professor of Digital Humanities at Carleton University. Together we have nearly forty years of experience with simulation and complex systems modeling of the Roman world.

With this project, we wanted to try some analog simulation of Romans instead. The Very First Map

The original map was sketched out using Visone network analysis software, and then modified in photoshop to adjust city node layout. We printed it out and then had it laminated at our local stationers so that we could play, adjust, argue, brainstorm, and play some more. It now hangs on Shawn’s office wall: the material culture of play!
